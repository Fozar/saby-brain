Navigation: [[index]] | [[Tensor-Company]] | [[Saby-Product-Lineup]]

## Summary

Internal Tensor knowledge-base document (Apr 4, 2026) listing all Saby development products, their verticals, responsible owners, tech leads, and QA engineers.

## Key Takeaways

- Saby = экосистема для бизнеса, единая линейка продуктов
- Products organized into ~15 verticals, each with an executive responsible
- Every product has: Ответственный / Тех.рук / Тестировщик
- Some verticals have only a responsible owner (no separate app listed)
