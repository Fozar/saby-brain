## Summary

Official Tensor naming guide (Jan 9, 2025) for all Saby products. Defines how to write "Saby", product names, mobile app names, and the complete СБИС → Saby renaming table.

## Key Rules

1. **"Saby"** — always Latin, first letter capitalized. Masculine gender in Russian.
2. **Product names** — always Latin. Format: `Saby <Word>` (second word capitalized).
3. **No Cyrillic-Latin mix**: не "Saby КЭДО", а "Saby Staff" или "КЭДО в Saby".
4. Products without their own app use just "Saby" (e.g., СБИС Маркировка → Saby).
